# Breakwater

Breakwater is a job-discovery and review platform designed to surface realistic early-career opportunities.

Job-board filters often label postings as entry level even when they require several years of experience. Remote listings may also be restricted to particular countries without making that restriction obvious. Breakwater addresses these problems by collecting permitted job data, separating required from preferred experience, evaluating Canadian remote eligibility, identifying nearby hybrid opportunities, and explaining why each posting is considered a match, possibility, stretch, unclear, or unsuitable.

## Initial goals

* Find remote opportunities open to Canadian applicants
* Find hybrid opportunities within a configurable distance
* Separate required from preferred experience
* Detect seniority and leadership requirements
* Preserve uncertain but potentially realistic opportunities
* Provide a focused daily job-review queue
* Explain important classification decisions

## Development strategy

The project begins as a local personal utility built with Django and PostgreSQL. After the workflow has been validated through real job searching, the same codebase will evolve into a documented portfolio product.

See [the master plan](docs/master-plan.md) for the complete product and implementation strategy.
